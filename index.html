Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your website

How long does it take to build my website? +

Most websites are completed within 1-2 weeks. Single page sites can be done in 3-5 days, while larger sites with multiple pages take 1-2 weeks depending on content and features.

Do I need to provide my own hosting and domain? +

No! I'll help you set up hosting and domain registration as part of your package. I'll guide you through the process and can even handle it for you if you prefer.

What if I need changes after my website is done? +

I offer monthly maintenance packages starting at $25/month for updates, or you can pay per update. I'm local, so I can make changes quickly and efficiently.

Will my website work on phones and tablets? +

Absolutely! All websites I build are mobile-first and responsive, meaning they look great and work perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers.

Do you work with businesses outside of Laurel, Whitley, and Knox counties? +

Yes! While I focus on local Kentucky businesses, I'm happy to work with anyone. Local businesses get priority and free consultations, but I serve clients statewide.

What's included in the free consultation? +

We'll discuss your business goals, what type of website you need, your budget, and I'll give you a detailed quote. No pressure, just honest advice about what's best for your business.

Can you help with content writing? +

Yes! I offer content writing services for $25-$45 per page. I can write about your business, services, and help you tell your story in a way that attracts customers.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes! I require a 50% deposit to start, and the remaining balance is due when your website is complete. For local businesses, I'm flexible with payment arrangements.

What makes you different from other web designers? +

I'm local, affordable, and I understand Kentucky businesses. I offer face-to-face meetings, quick turnaround times, and pricing that works for small businesses. Plus, I'm always available for support.

Will my website show up on Google? +

Yes! All websites include basic SEO optimization to help you show up in local searches. I can also set up your Google My Business listing to help customers find you.
